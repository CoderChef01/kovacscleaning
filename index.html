About

Kovacs Cleaning has been on market as a family business since 2019. ;
Since the beginning we always wanted to create a business which offered outstanding services to all of our happy customers.
Our motto is that "if you always treat your client the way you'd want to be treated the business will succeed"
All customers are dealt with us personally, like I mentioned earlier this business is ran by only and exclusively
our family this is why we are called "Kovacs Cleaning"!
